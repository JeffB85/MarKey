About Us

Who are we

MarKey is an Intellectual Property consultancy. We are specialized in the protection of trademarks, and the design of products and trade names. Thanks to an international network, we can protect your rights anywhere in the world. We examine brands for availability and manage and monitor national and international brand portfolios. With our trademark management system you have an up-to-date overview of all your registrations at any time. We also deal with and advise on conflicts and oppositions. Our advice is concrete, practical and in understandable language.

MarKey was founded in 2021 by Mark Cawllins, a trademark specialist with more than 15 years of experience at various national and international IP practices. Mark Cawllins is registered as European Trademark and Design Attorney.
